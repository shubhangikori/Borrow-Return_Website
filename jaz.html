JAZBAAT

Feeling of expressions Drama is the specific mode of fiction represented in performance. Jazbaat, the dramatics club of IIITDM Jabalpur, is one of the prominent cultural clubs of the college. The logo of Jazbaat,i.e., the two masks associated with drama represents the two sides of the drama, the Comedy and the Tragedy.

“The need for a Dramatics Club met with fruitful results when the club was officially announced on 15th August 2011,as Jazbaat. The aim was to tap upcoming talent and tuning the existing potential among students. The Dramatics Club of IIITDMJ is a perfect platform for budding artistes to honor their skills. The club provides an opportunity for students to actively participate in all aspects of play production.” The club consists of the students from both under-graduate and post-graduate sections of the college. The club is run under cultural counsel of the college and consists of the office bearers from the students only.The club had 20 members when it started and the count has reached to 68 since then. Generally , there are four forms of drama that we usually perform and they are : Nukkad (Street Play) , One act play(stage play) , Mime (silent act) and ad-mad (comedy stage advertisements).
